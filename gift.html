Christmas 2023: The holiday where my online gift-buying luck ran out. 😂

The day after Thanksgiving, I saw this and thought you might like it. I saw the seller was in the Ukraine and was optimistic it would get here on time regardless. Ordered it. It shipped. I watched the tracking anxiously as it “sat in Kiev” for 3 weeks.

Then, suddenly, it was in White Plains on 12/21! and then Middletown on 12/22! It would get here on time! Huzzah!

Except that the seller sent it registered mail without telling me… the mail carrier showed up at 10:47am on December 23, needing a signature… and I was out grocery shopping.

😭😭😭
